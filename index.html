About The Universal Hip Hop Parade

The UHHP takes place on the streets of Bed-Stuy on August 17th, 2019 starting at 11AM. Come out and be a part of the worldâ€™s first social justice hip-hop parade. Held to celebrate the birthday of the great social justice activist and freedom fighter Marcus Garvey, this family friendly event will provide a moving platform for elements of hip-hop, art and culture combined with a progressive social justice message.

What UHHP Stands For




The UHHP celebrates the legacy of Marcus Garvey and the creativity of hip-hop culture.





Hip-Hop

The UHHP Organization celebrates and embraces the 5 pillars of Hip-Hop: MCing, DJing, Breakdance, Graffiti, and Knowledge. We are commited to promoting the growth of Hip-Hop as well as celebrating it's past and present to pave way for the future.

Marcus Garvey

The UHHP is influenced by the philosophy of Marcus Garvey. We believe and adhere to the idea of social and economic empowerment for people of color.
