About The Universal Hip Hop Parade

The UHHP takes place on the streets of Bed-Stuy on August 17th, 2019 starting at 11am. Come out and be a part of the worldâ€™s first social justice hip-hop parade Held to celebrate the birthday of the great social justice activist and freedom fighter Marcus Garvey, this family friendly event will provide a moving platform for elements of hip-hop art and culture combined with progressive social justice messaging.

What UHHP Stands For

The UHHP celebrates the legacy of Marcus Garvey and the creativity of hip-hop culture.
